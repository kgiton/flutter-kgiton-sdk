## Obtaining a License

### Step 1: Submit License Request

Contact PT KGiTON via email with your license request:

**Email:** support@kgiton.com  
**Subject:** KGiTON SDK License Request

**Required Information:**
- Company/Organization name
- Contact person name and title
- Business email address
- Phone number
- Intended use case (brief description)
- Estimated project timeline
- Number of developers who will use the SDK
- Expected number of devices/deployments

### Step 2: Review License Agreement

Upon receiving your request, PT KGiTON will provide:

1. **Commercial License Agreement** - Legal terms and conditions
2. **Pricing Information** - Based on your requirements
3. **Service Level Agreement (SLA)** - Support and maintenance terms
4. **Technical Requirements** - Integration specifications

Review all documents carefully before proceeding.

### Step 3: Complete Agreement

To finalize your license:

1. Sign the Commercial License Agreement
2. Complete payment according to agreed terms
3. Provide necessary business documentation (if required)
4. Confirm billing and contact information
